ABOUTME

John Currey is an artist from Middletown, Rhode Island, just north of Newport. He is currently pursuing a degree in Animation and Visual Effects at Purdue University. He has had a passion for art for his entire life and has been sketching for as long as he can remember. He has recently been studying and exploring new mediums, such as 3D modeling and visual effects work. His dream is to work as a cartoonist. He is inspired by artists like Genndy Tartakovsky and Benjy Brooke.
